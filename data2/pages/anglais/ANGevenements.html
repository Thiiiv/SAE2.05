Events

Holy day

Annual festival, at the beginning of April

Colorful show that celebrates the culture and religion of the city. The celebration begins with a procession of the main religious figures of the city, accompanied by music and dance performances. The procession then moves to the cathedral where people can admire people can admire the statues and religious objects. At the end, music and dance performances follow in the streets of the city.

Patio Festival

Annual festival, mid-May

Competitions for the decoration of patios, or interior courtyards, which take place in the historic historic districts of the city. The patios are decorated with flowers, plants, and trellis arches. creating colorful and inviting spaces. The Patio Festival is considered one of the one of the most important events of the year in Cordoba.

Christmas

Annual party, at the end of December

Christmas in Cordoba is celebrated mainly as a religious and family event. People usually family and friends to celebrate the birth of Jesus. The streets of Cordoba are decorated with lights and decorations, Christmas markets are organized in the city organized in the city, where visitors can buy gifts, decorations and local and local specialties.

Cross of May

Annual festival, at the end of April

This festival consists in installing in the streets and squares of the city, crosses made of flowers. These creations also participate in a competition organized by the municipality. It It is an ancient religious tradition. It is the neighborhood associations that are responsible to make Cordoba a picture of color and celebration. The crosses are exposed all day, and in the evening the atmosphere is provided by shows of music and dance
